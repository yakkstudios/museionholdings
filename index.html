The House

Museion Holdings is the legal name. House of Museion is the ceremonial one. Both are correct.

Museion Holdings (Limited) is the corporate parent — the entity that holds the IP, the equity, the cap table, the contracts, the relationships with banks and counsel and investors. It's the name on the paperwork.

House of Museion is the brand above the brands — the ceremonial layer that the Athenaeum suite, the Gentlemen of Muse, the scrolls, and every storytelling moment carry. It's the name on the seal.

Underneath sit four operating opcos (YAKK Studios, Museion Labs, KAIZE Labs, MINT Foundation), one community order (Gentlemen of Muse), one chain venue ($MUSE on Ethereum L2 → eventual MUSE L1), and a small family hospitality wing (Johnny Boy's Wines · Ali's of Aberdeen) carried forward from Jay's grandparents.
